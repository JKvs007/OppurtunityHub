Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

OpportunityHub collects minimal data necessary to provide our search and bookmarking features. We store saved opportunities locally on your device via localStorage. No personal identification details are automatically compiled or uploaded to a database.

2. Use of Information

Any details entered locally (e.g., searches, simulated profiles, saved jobs) remain completely client-side. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your local browsing state to third parties.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage to store your saved opportunities and bookmarks. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cookies and site data.
